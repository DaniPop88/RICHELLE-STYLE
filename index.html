About Richelle Style Wardrobe

Founded by Richelle Manalo in 2022, Richelle Style Wardrobe began as a small online shop and quickly became a trusted clothing boutique in Imus, Cavite. Our passion is making fashion expressive, empowering, and accessible to every Filipina.

  • Everyday wear with flair
  • Smart casual & workwear staples
  • Size-inclusive, comfortable fashion
  • Statement pieces for all occasions

Support local, wear local, love local.

FAQs

  • Q: Do you have a physical store?

  • Q: Do you accept walk-in customers?

    A: Yes, we deliver anywhere in the Philippines via J&T and LBC.
